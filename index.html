📖 How to Use

🐠 Placing Creatures
• Tap a creature button to start staging it
• Drag to position; corner handles to resize & rotate
• ↔ Flip to mirror the image horizontally
• Layer slider — slide left for back, right for front
• ✅ Place It! to confirm — staging continues with a small offset
• A cooldown applies between placements to prevent spam
🔍 Navigation
• Pinch to zoom in/out (mobile)
• Scroll wheel to zoom in/out (desktop)
• Drag empty canvas to pan the view
• Reset View button resets zoom & pan
↩ Undo
• Undo Last removes the most recent creature you placed
⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts
• Ctrl+Shift+H — Toggle display/kiosk mode (hides all UI)
• Ctrl+Shift+T — Open teacher mode
• Esc — Close any open modal
🎓 Teacher Mode
• Access via the 🎓 icon at the bottom of the sidebar, or Ctrl+Shift+T
• Lock/unlock placements for all students
• Show/hide overlay image controls
• Clear the canvas for everyone
